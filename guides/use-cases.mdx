# Use Cases

This guide shows practical use cases for the Blockline MEV Analytics API. More detailed content coming soon.

## Front-Running Detection

Analyze transactions that occurred before yours in the same slot to detect potential front-running.

## Sandwich Attack Detection

Identify if your transaction was sandwiched by detecting matching signers before and after your trade.

## Competition Analysis

Study competitor behavior and transaction patterns in your target markets.

## Speed Ranking

Compare transaction timing and PoH tick offsets to understand execution speed advantages.

## Pattern Recognition

Identify recurring MEV patterns and bot behavior in specific markets.

## Financial Impact Analysis

Calculate the financial impact of MEV activity on your trades using balance change data.
